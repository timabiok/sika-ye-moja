Project Overview

Vaco will provide a senior consultant from our Cloud Infrastructure Practice to deliver advisory, architectural guidance, and migration support services toward Client's transition from Ubuntu to Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL).

Scope of Work
Vaco will provide support for migration, execution, and cutover activities including the following steps:

1. Migration Planning and Technical Guidance
2. RHEL Standards & Best Practices Development
3. Risk Identification and Mitigation
4. Environment Improvement Recommendations
5. Cutover Support and Post-Implementation Troubleshooting
6. Knowledge Transfer & Support Planning

Assumptions

Client Responsibilities
•	Client will provide timely access to systems, documentation, and required personnel.
•	Client will designate a primary decision-maker for approvals and coordination.
•	Client is responsible for obtaining all necessary RHEL licensing.
•	Client will ensure appropriate backups are completed prior to production changes.

Environment & Technical
•	Ubuntu environments exist and are stable at project start.
•	Infrastructure and hardware are compatible with RHEL requirements.
•	Application dependencies and compatibility risks will be identified by Client.
•	Application remediation beyond Operating System upgrade is out of scope.

Scope Boundaries
•	This engagement is advisory in nature. Client will retain ownership of execution and cutover activities, with Vaco providing structured guidance, validation, and technical oversight support.
•	Services are limited to planning and executing the Ubuntu to RHEL upgrade.
•	Ongoing operational support is not included unless separately agreed.
•	Additional security, monitoring, or automation enhancements are out of scope unless defined.
